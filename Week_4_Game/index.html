How To Play
You will be given a random number at the start of each game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score with the random number and lose the game if you go over the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. You have to remember it.

Each time when the game starts, the crystal's value will change.
